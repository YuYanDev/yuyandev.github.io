YuYan Research Network

YuYan Research Network AS207292 is an amateur research network. Built in March 2021. The network is mainly used for experimental research on computer networks, and carries the network traffic of the YuYan Platform.

YuYan Research Network currently has Internet service facilities in the following regions:
